Overview

  1. Begin with your week 08-09 Unity Project (this needs all implementation so far)
  2. Work from CW1 Unity Project or Tutorial 08 Base Project provided and build all week 8 and 9 features.
  3. This week is about completing the interaction loop for one diner prefab.
  4. After this, multiple diners, animations, and even the Diner List can be used for extra marks.
